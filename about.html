About University LMS

The University Learning Management System (ULMS) is a web-based platform designed to support students and lecturers by providing an online learning environment. Students can access lecture notes, download assignments, manage their profiles, and stay updated with academic resources from one place.


Project Objectives

  • Provide easy access to lecture materials.
  • Allow students to download assignments.
  • Maintain student profile information.
  • Create a simple and secure learning platform.
  • Improve communication between students and lecturers.
